Executive Summary

The Problem

  • Travel managers struggle to enforce complex policies
  • Travelers face overwhelming choice and decision paralysis
  • Low policy compliance (65-75%) and traveler frustration
  • Existing systems: too rigid (rules) or too opaque (black-box ML)

The Solution

  • LLM-powered policy parsing from natural language
  • Multi-objective recommendation ranking (compliance + cost + loyalty + comfort)
  • Confidence scoring for transparency and trust
  • Feedback loops for continuous improvement
